About Cochin Laughter Yoga

Cochin Laughter Yoga, established in May 2011 is a movement for tension free world. It aims at spreading the message of health through laugh. The well known business man and social icon Mr. Kochouseph Chittilappilly was the founder of this move and at that time there were only 4 members to join with him. The activities and training sessions were held at Kochi Jawaharlal Nehru Intl. staduim. Later many volunteers and members willingly joined hands with this yoga therapy and now we have become a large bunch comprising people from different levels of society irrespective of caste, religion, education, age group and gender.

Apart from training sessions we are also doing various charity activities for humanity and man kind. Social gatherings among members are also a usual part of us. We welcome you all to join with us. Come on let's make a difference...

Laughter Yoga Therapy

This practice teaches you how to laugh without using jokes and provides a safe space for you to tap into your inner child by letting go of judgement, negativity, fear and stress and replacing those feelings with love, peace, acceptance and joy. We welcome all, the training is absolutely free.

Benefits of Laughter Yoga

Members are getting better health benefits and fun with friends. "Laughter" yoga gives good excercise and strengthens immune system reducing stress and worry. The yoga session provides movements and excercise to almost every part of our body which make us flexible and powerful.
