About

Who We Are?

Cochin Laughter Yoga, established in May 2011 is a movement for tension free world. It aims at spreading the message of health through laugh. The well known business man and social icon Mr. Kochouseph Chittilappilly was the founder of this move and at that time there were only 4 members to join with him. The activities and training sessions were held at Kochi Jawaharlal Nehru Intl. staduim.

Later many volunteers and members willingly joined hands with this yoga therapy and now we have become a large bunch comprising people from different levels of society irrespective of caste, religion, education, age group and gender.

How we rate us!

Our Resources Laughter yoga training Quality of trainers Personal development Health and attitude Bond of love among us

Apart from training sessions we are also doing various charity activities for humanity and man kind. Social gatherings among members are also a usual part of us. We used to visit he houses of the members of our group and consoles at needy times. We ourselves stood for each other raising a helping hand at any moment. We also used to make the functions among us a festival. We used to celebrate together almost all events like Onam, XMas, NewYear, anniversaries etc.

Laughter yoga teaches you how to laugh without using jokes and provides a safe space for you to tap into your inner child by letting go of judgement, negativity, fear and stress and replacing those feelings with love, peace, acceptance and joy.
