Our Activities

What we do?

Free training sessions on laughter yoga therapy.

Sessions on Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday every week
Time between 7 AM and 8 AM
Venue @ Jawaharlal Nehru International Stadium, Kaloor, Kochi
For more details contact us

Building a better lovable society to live in !

Laughter yoga teaches you how to laugh without using jokes and provides a safe space for you to tap into your inner child by letting go of judgement, negativity, fear and stress and replacing those feelings with love, peace, acceptance and joy.

We would like to reach you.

People from resident associations, schools, colleges and other industrial institutions can come and join us for Laughter Yoga session. The admission and membership is absolutely free. The participants will be getting free training and can start their own branches of laughter yoga club if interested.

Apart from training sessions we are also doing various charity activities for humanity and man kind. Social gatherings among members are also a usual part of us.
